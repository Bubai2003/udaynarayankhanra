SARASWATI PUJO
Every year our club conducts this Saraswati Puja, which started auspiciously on 14th. We all together will do this Saraswati Puja every year. So all of you stay by our side so that we can holdabiggerpuja.
Ya Kundendu-Tushara-Hara-Dhavala, Ya Shubhra-Vastravrita, Ya Vina-Vara-Danda-Mandita-Kara, Ya Shveta-Padmasana. Ya Brahmachyuta-Shankara-Prabhritibhir Devaih Sada Vandita, Sa Mam Patu Saraswati Bhagawati Nihshesha-Jadyapaha.
Saraswati Pujo, the worship of Goddess Saraswati -- the Goddess of Learning in Hindu Mythology, is a special occasion in Bengal. Every year on the day of Vasanta Panchami the Goddess is worshipped in numerous Bengali homes and also in schools, colleges and socialcommunities.

SOCIAL WORK
double_arrow We give blankets every year to the elderly people of our club's area, so that every elderly person feels comfortable. We will try to alleviate the suffering of our club by donating more necessary things to the people ofseveralareas.
dictionary From the side of the club Books, notebooks, pencils, pens, etc. are distributed to the talented and poor students of two villages. So that every boy and girl can study well, it is our goal that no boy or girl in the village remainsuneducated.
bloodtype Our club organizes this Blood Donation Camp every year during Saraswati Puja, managed by Sahayatri Club. We hand over these donating blood to Hospital, so that they can helpsickpeople.
Blood donation is safe. New, sterile disposable equipment is used for each donor, so there's no risk of getting a bloodborne infection by donating blood. Most healthy adults can donate a pint (about half a liter) safely, without health risks. Within a few days of a blood donation, your body replaces thelostfluids.
